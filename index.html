Terms of Service

Swoon!TM is a dating-sim where your actions are determined by a roll of the dice. After our flawless algorithm sets you up on your perfect date, you will be led through a series of events. How you respond to each situation is determined by a dice roll attempt.

When faced with a decision moment, click "Roll the dice!" to generate a random roll. You are allowed three attempts at each roll and you can even lock in faces you'd like to keep between attempts! When you're happy with your roll - or you're out of attempts (it's okay, we all get nervous and fumble over our words) - click "Make your move!" to advance.

The symbol showing on the majority of your dice during your last attempt will determine how you act. (A random choice will be made if there is a tie; indecision does not facilitate romance!) The result will affect the opinion of your date. More matching faces means a stronger move. Do well enough and you'll earn a second date!

One last thing... The signature Swoon!TM heart represents little moments of unspoken flirting. Each one showing when you "Make your move!" will fill the Swoon-O-Meter!TM Five hearts will trigger a special swoonworthy moment at the end of your date that can tip the scales in your favor! The moment will also be better the stronger your date feels.

Dating Tip: There's nothing wrong with flirting, but it won't mean much without substance to back it up. Just because you have chemistry doesn't mean you've made a personal connection. A great date has both. Balance them well and you'll get that second date!
