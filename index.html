Swoon!TM is a dating-sim where your actions are determined by a roll of the dice. After our flawless algorithm sets you up on your perfect date, you will be led through a series of events. How you respond to each situation is determined by a dice roll attempt.

When faced with a decision moment, click "Roll the dice!" to generate a random roll. You are allowed three attempts at each roll and you can even lock in faces you'd like to keep between rolls! When you are happy with your roll - or you're out of attempts (it's okay, we all get nervous and fumble over our words) - click "Make your move!" to lock it in.

The symbol showing on the majority of your dice will determine how you act. (A random choice will be made if there is a tie; indecision does not facilitate romance!) The result will affect the opinion of your date. Depending on the event and their personality, certain moves will be more effective than others.
