Terms of Service

Swoon!TM is a dating-sim where your actions are determined by a roll of the dice. After our flawless algorithm sets you up on your perfect date, you will be led through a series of events. How you respond to each situation is determined by a dice roll attempt.

When faced with a decision moment, click "Roll the dice!" to generate a random roll. You are allowed three attempts at each roll and you can even lock in faces you'd like to keep between attempts! When you're happy with your roll - or you're out of attempts (it's okay, we all get nervous and fumble over our words) - click "Make your move!" to advance.

The symbol showing on the majority of your dice during your last attempt will determine how you act. (A random choice will be made if there is a tie; indecision does not facilitate romance!) The result will affect the opinion of your date. More matching faces means a stronger move. Depending on the event and their personality, certain moves will even be more effective than others. Do well enough and you'll earn a second date!

Funny ~ Impress your date with your sense of humor!

Clever ~ Impress your date with your dazzling intellect!

Sweet ~ Impress your date with your more sensitive side!

Swoon ~ Fill your Swoon-O-Meter!TM to make a BIG impression!

One last thing... When you "Make your move!", any dice showing the signature Swoon!TM heart will add one heart to the Swoon-O-Meter!TM When this meter hits five hearts, it will trigger a swoonworthy moment that will score you BIG points with your date! Be careful, though. The heart is not worth any points by itself, so choose to lock them in wisely.
